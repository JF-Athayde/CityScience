Project Summary

Our project is a versatile data platform designed to transform complex environmental and urban data into actionable insights for decision-makers. The system generates customized, interactive technical bulletins that address critical challenges in urban development. Its capabilities range from conducting detailed geotechnical risk assessments to enhance the safety and resilience of infrastructure, to creating comprehensive sustainability plans that guide construction projects toward certifications like LEED. By translating raw data into clear, evidence-based recommendations, the platform empowers engineers, planners, and city officials to build safer, more resilient, and truly sustainable¬†communities.

‚ö†Ô∏è The Problem

Rapid and unplanned urban expansion is a primary driver of global environmental challenges. Cities consume over two-thirds of the world's energy and are responsible for more than 70% of global CO2 emissions, significantly contributing to climate change and resource depletion. These global pressures manifest as severe local problems that directly impact human quality of life. In many metropolitan areas, disordered growth creates critical socio-environmental issues, such as the Urban Heat Island (UHI) effect, which intensifies heatwaves, and severe air quality degradation. Furthermore, this unplanned expansion often pushes vulnerable populations into high-risk areas, exposing them to climate-related disasters and deepening social¬†inequalities.
